Who am I?

I am a 20 year-old student at Thomas More. I came to Belgium around 2 years ago and have been living here ever since then. Programming and the field of Computer Science in general has been a part of my life ever since middle school. It eventually became my career path as I'm studying Applied Computer Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence. I currently live in a student house in Geel, but originally I live in Luik with my family.

Ever since a young age, I've lived an international life. I experienced learning and working with people from around the world, which is also the reason I know English really well and consider myself bilingual in it. I've lived in Malaysia as a kid, in Palestine which is my home country, in the USA on exchange, in Turkey, and now Belgium. All these experiences made me adaptable to change and curious to try new things. It definitely enhanced my communication skills with people of different cultures too.
