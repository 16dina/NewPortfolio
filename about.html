Who am I?

I am a 20 year-old student at Thomas More. I came to Belgium around 2 years ago and have been living here ever since then. Programming and the field of Computer Science in general has been a part of my life ever since middle school. It eventually became my career path as I'm studying Applied Computer Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence. I currently live in a student house in Geel, but originally I live in Luik with my family.

Ever since a young age, I've lived an international life. I experienced learning and working with people from around the world, which is also the reason I know English really well and consider myself bilingual in it. I've lived in Malaysia as a kid, in Palestine which is my home country, in the USA on exchange, in Turkey, and now Belgium. All these experiences made me adaptable to change and curious to try new things. It definitely enhanced my communication skills with people of different cultures too.

My Timeline

2016 - 2019

Code For Palestine - Paltel Group Foundation

This program was part of Code.X, in it I started my journey in Computer Science. Mentors from Stanfor University volunteered to come teach students in Gaza, Palestine about Computer Science and Design Thinking. It was an enriching experience where I learned a lot and grew as a person. Feel free to check out the program here.

2017 - 2018

Technovation Girls

I participated in the Technovation challenge with a team. It is a program that aims to encourage young women to get into STEM. We came up with an idea to make an application that connects students with university professors and people who work in a major that a student might be interested in. This is to help them get to know more about the field and decide whether to pursue it or not and what tips they should follow. Check out more about Technovation here.

2018 - 2019

Kennedy-Lugar Youth Exchange and Study (YES) - US Department of State

I was among the lucky few in my year from Gaza, Palestine, who got the chance to do a fully funded exchange year in the United States. I stayed in Michigan for 10 months with a host family, and participated in many activities and clubs. Check out more about the scholarship here.

2021 - 2024

Thomas More Hogeschool

Currently, I am studying Applied Computer Science in Belgium. I'm attending Thomas More Hogeschool in Geel. My study trajectory is Applied Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence). I am also part of the student council for the IT Factory which consists of students who are following IT related courses.
